Hello everyone, I am now starting to reorganize my website. Previously, it was not well maintained, and many of the contents were incomplete. Now, with the help of AI, many technical challenges have become much easier to solve. For example, this website is built using Jekyll, and while reorganizing it, I encountered some technical issues. With AI assistance, problems that might have taken several hours to resolve can now be solved in just tens of minutes.

Another point is that I now write the website content in my native language, Chinese, and then use AI to translate it into English. This approach ensures high translation accuracy, and writing in my native language significantly improves both my efficiency and the quality of my content. Previously, my English writing was often rough and unpolished, but now AI-generated translations provide a well-refined version.

The reason I want to improve this personal website is that I want to promote myself through it. I know that the microfluidics skills and experience I have are valuable, and I want more people to learn about my expertise and background. This could potentially lead to future collaborations. I also plan to include a link to this website in my CV, so HR or recruiters can gain a deeper understanding of me.

In the future, I have also considered entrepreneurship—using microfluidics technology to start a company. However, this is still in the conceptual and experimental stage, and it will depend on how demanding my new postdoctoral position turns out to be.
